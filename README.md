### 2. Key Architecture: The Dual-Loop Simulation Engine üîÑ

The platform orchestrates two parallel **simulation loops** to decouple public information dissemination from internal operational dynamics.

* **The Deterministic Stream (Public Layer):**
    Powered by `helpers_public.ts` and the `GlobalContext`, this engine filters volatility to provide a **stable data feed**. It ensures citizens receive **actionable availability metrics** without the confusing noise of micro-fluctuations.

* **The Stochastic Stream (Strategic Layer):**
    Managed by `simulationEngine.ts` and the `StrategicContext`, this engine models **non-linear variables**‚Äîspecifically staff fatigue and bed occupancy rates. It simulates health systems strain, where (e.g.) surging occupancy escalates emergency wait times.

> **State Synchronisation:** The system features **bi-directional data flow**. A manual override by a Nodal Officer in the **Hospital Portal** instantly propagates system-wide, demonstrating real-time responsiveness to human intervention.

### 3. Data Foundation üìä

Underpinning the platform is a **stratified pan-Indian dataset** of 150 hospitals, **manually curated** from **official hospital websites, government health bulletins, and public health directories** to ensure high fidelity.
